The company promoters wanted to offer high-quality cheap products through the e-commerce channel. This was possible thanks to the optimization of the supplies and communications and of the transport and thanks to reduction in prices, which are typical of the traditional market. The choice paid off: nowadays the Agritech Store can boast a standing increase in the e-commerce's sales both in Italy and abroad.

Advantage

For all the Agritech Store Store's products there is an official guarantee of the maker, which is validated by the invoice. In addition, this document is legally valid and it accompanies all the goods.
Agritech Store provides the registered and the not-registered customers with a complete list of the Italian service centres, in order to simplify the requests of spare parts and technical assistance in general.
As regards the online purchases, Agritech Store makes use of the best security programs, in order to guarantee sure purchases to the customers. In case of payment through a credit card, the direct connection with our banking systems guarantees the security during the purchases.
Agritech Store only deals with high- quality cheap articles, in order to guarantee the final customer the security to buy a reliable product, which can last more than others, and whose spare parts are easily found for many years in the whole national territory.
